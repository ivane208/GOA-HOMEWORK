About Asus

Asus, officially known as ASUSTeK Computer Inc., is a multinational computer hardware and electronics c ompany based in Taiwan. The company was founded in 1989 by four individuals, namely T.H. Tung, Ted Hsu, Wayne Hsieh, and M.T. Liao. Asus has since grown to become one of the world's leading manufacturers of computer hardware,
including motherboards, graphics cards, laptops, desktops, monitors,
networking equipment, and more.
